Our story

Built by women, for women.

The Galhood was created by Parul Yadav and Mansi Chaudhary, two women navigating their 20s and realizing how hard it can be to find meaningful connections and a community that truly feels supportive.

Parul's story is shaped by years of moving, exploring, and building a life far from home. After moving to Toronto, she traveled solo extensively and worked across the US, seeing just how powerful community can be when you're starting over and chasing growth.

Mansi brings her perspective from working in the financial sector within India's growing startup ecosystem. With an MBA from NMIMS, Mumbai, she understands ambition, pressure, and what it takes to build a career you actually care about.

Together they created The Galhood, a place where women can connect, learn, support one another, and grow without judgment. What started as a shared need for community turned into a global collective built on honesty, encouragement, and showing up for each other.

Meet the team

The people behind it

Parul Yadav

Co-founder

Parul is a communications professional and first-generation immigrant who moved to Toronto from India and has since built a life across Canada and the US, working across Arizona, Colorado, Illinois, and Ohio. She travels solo extensively and saw firsthand how powerful real community can be when you're starting over and figuring things out. She currently runs her own integrated media agency, North Wave Media, where connection and storytelling are at the heart of everything she does. She co-founded The Galhood because she wanted to build the community she always wished existed.

Mansi Chaudhary

Co-founder

Mansi curates all of The Galhood's in-person and virtual events spanning career, finance, wellness, tech, entrepreneurship, and more. With an MBA from NMIMS, Mumbai, and a background in India's growing startup ecosystem, she understands ambition and what it takes to build a career you actually care about. She is also a passionate social media creator with a strong aesthetic eye who loves bringing brands and communities to life.
